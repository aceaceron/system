About La Casa De Espeso

Welcome to La Casa de Espeso, your serene retreat in the heart of Labo, Camarines Norte. Opened last 2023, we have welcomed hundreds and counting customer in our paradise. Our charming hotel, with its distinctive turquoise facade, offers a welcoming atmosphere for both leisure and business travelers. We pride ourselves on exceptional hospitality, tight security and personalized service. Our spacious rooms feature cozy bedding, adequate bathing area and relaxing experience. La Casa de Espeso is the perfect base for exploring Camarines Norte. Our concierge team is ready to ensure your stay is seamless and enjoyable. Experience comfort, luxury, and convenience at La Casa de Espeso. We look forward to welcoming you!
